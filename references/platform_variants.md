# Platform Variants

Generate platform variants only after the route decision.

## Normal WeChat

Purpose:
- Learning influence
- Method sharing
- Personal knowledge asset

Style:
- Structured long-form Chinese article
- More complete reasoning
- Clear learning takeaway

## Enterprise WeChat

Purpose:
- Demand generation
- Trust building
- Decision-maker education

Style:
- Business problem first
- Conditions, ROI evidence or validation plan, risk, objection
- One lead-capture action

## Xiaohongshu

Purpose:
- High-density takeaway
- Save-worthy note
- Reader problem awareness

Structure:
- Title with one sharp insight
- 3-5 points
- One checklist
- Optional tags

Do not make Xiaohongshu a shortened WeChat article.

## Short Video Script

Purpose:
- Explain one insight quickly
- Drive comment, private message, or diagnostic action

Structure:
- Hook
- Problem
- Key judgment
- Example or test result
- One action

Keep the spoken script simple and concrete.
