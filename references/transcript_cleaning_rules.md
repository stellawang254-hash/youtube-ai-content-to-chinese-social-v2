# Transcript Cleaning Rules

The cleaning layer should make the transcript more usable for learning, not prettier for publishing.

## Allowed

- remove repeated subtitle lines
- remove numbering and timestamps
- remove obvious filler words
- normalize known technical terms
- merge broken lines into readable sentences
- separate by topic when it helps analysis

## Not Allowed

- rewrite the source into a polished article
- add missing claims
- smooth away uncertainty that matters
- over-edit technical terms you are not sure about

## Output Goal

The cleaned transcript should be good enough to become a learning note, not a final article.
