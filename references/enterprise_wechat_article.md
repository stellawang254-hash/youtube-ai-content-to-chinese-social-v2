# Enterprise WeChat Article Route

Use this route for demand-generation articles around enterprise AI implementation.

## Required Source

Use the enterprise opportunity brief, not raw YouTube material.

Do not create enterprise content by adding an ROI paragraph, risk paragraph, or lead-capture action to the end of a Route 1 learning article.

## Article Must Include

- Enterprise problem
- Target decision maker
- Business scenario
- Applicable conditions
- Non-applicable conditions
- Known investment and return evidence, or an ROI hypothesis and measurement plan
- Implementation risks
- Practical result or validation path
- Author judgment
- Counterargument
- One lead-capture action

## Suggested Structure

```md
# 标题

## 开头：从企业真实问题切入

## 这个问题为什么不是简单上 AI 就能解决

## 我从这条内容里得到的启发

## 我实际试了什么 / 会怎么验证

## 放到企业里，适合的业务场景是什么

## 什么条件下值得做

## 投入和收益大概怎么算

没有数据时，写成 ROI 假设和验证计划，不要写成确定收益。

## 最大的落地风险

## 我的判断：先做小验证，不要直接做大系统

## 反对意见：什么时候不建议做

## 线索承接动作
```

## Lead-Capture Rule

Each article gets one lead-capture action only.

The action must match the article:
- Diagnostic article -> invite process diagnosis
- Checklist article -> invite checklist download or comment keyword
- Case article -> invite scenario consultation
- Risk article -> invite risk self-check

Do not attach generic sales copy.

## Voice Rules

- Be specific, not grand.
- Use "I tested" only when the user supplied a real test. Otherwise prefer "I would first check / this should be validated by / I do not recommend".
- Keep trust higher than conversion pressure.

## No-Fabrication Rules

Never invent:
- personal tests
- client experience
- project results
- numerical ROI
- cost savings
- conversion gains
- implementation cases

When evidence is missing, state the missing evidence and provide a validation path.
