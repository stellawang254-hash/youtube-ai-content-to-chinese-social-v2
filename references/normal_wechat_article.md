# Normal WeChat Article Route

Use this route when the source is suitable for public learning content but not necessarily enterprise AI implementation advice.

## Inputs

Required:
- Chinese learning note
- Method summary
- Author judgment

Optional:
- Practical result
- Personal observation
- Xiaohongshu or video variant request

## Article Requirements

The article must:
- Be useful without watching the original video
- Start from a Chinese reader problem or learning confusion
- Include the author's judgment
- Include at least one limitation or objection
- Avoid direct translation and raw summary

## Suggested Structure

```md
# 标题

## 开头：从一个学习问题切入

## 这条内容真正有价值的地方

## 我看完后的判断

## 可以直接借鉴的方法

## 我不建议照搬的地方

## 如果你想自己试，可以从这几步开始

## 结尾：回到学习价值
```

## Not Enterprise by Default

Do not add business scenarios, ROI, or lead-capture framing unless the route decision says the enterprise route fits.
